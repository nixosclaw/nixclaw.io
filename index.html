Our Solutions Are Built Different

Powered by NixOS

Our infrastructure is built on NixOS — a fully reproducible operating system where every server, agent, and deployment is defined in code. No configuration drift. Instant rollbacks. What works in testing is guaranteed to work in production.

Isolated by MicroVMs

Why NixOS?

NixOS is a Linux operating system built on a radically different principle: every system configuration is defined in code, versioned, and reproducible. This means your entire infrastructure — from servers to AI agents — can be rebuilt identically at any time, on any machine.

For your business, this means:

No Configuration Drift

Traditional servers degrade over time as patches, updates, and manual fixes accumulate. NixOS eliminates this entirely. Your production environment today is identical to your production environment six months from now.

Instant Rollback

A bad deployment? Roll back to the previous working state in seconds — not hours. NixOS keeps every previous version of your system ready to restore.

Security by Design

NixOS isolates every component of your system. Combined with microVM sandboxing, your AI agents run in ephemeral containers that are destroyed after each task. Even if an agent is compromised, it cannot reach your core systems.

Reproducible Everywhere

Deploy the same configuration to your laptop, your staging server, and your production cluster. What works in testing is guaranteed to work in production. No surprises.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NixOS and do I need to understand it?

NixOS is the operating system we use to build your infrastructure. You do not need to understand it — that is our job. What it means for you is that your systems are more reliable, more secure, and easier to maintain than anything built on traditional Linux.

Can you work with Claude, ChatGPT, Codex, or other frontier models?

Yes. We are model-agnostic. We integrate whichever AI model best fits your use case — whether that is a cloud-based frontier model like Claude or ChatGPT, or a fully private local model running on your own hardware.

We start with a free 30-minute audit to understand your current workflows and identify quick wins. From there, we propose a fixed-scope implementation — typically a 1-4 week sprint to deploy your AI agents and infrastructure. Ongoing support retainers are available after delivery.

How do you keep our data private?

We offer two approaches. For maximum privacy, we deploy local AI models on your own hardware — your data never leaves your network. For cloud-based models, we implement strict policies, access controls, and sandboxed execution environments to minimize exposure.
